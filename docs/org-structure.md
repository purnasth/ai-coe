## How the Chatbot Uses the Chart

- When you ask about company structure, people, or projects, the chatbot retrieves this chart and its summary to provide both a visual and a textual answer.
- In a web interface, the image will be displayed inline. In a terminal, the image path will be shown for manual viewing.
- The summary and details above help the chatbot answer questions about roles, teams, and organizational layout, even if the image cannot be displayed directly.
